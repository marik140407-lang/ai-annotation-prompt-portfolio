VPN Answer Evaluation Example

**User question:**  
"Explain what a VPN is in simple terms for a non-technical user."

## Model Answer A

"A VPN is a virtual private network that uses encryption and tunneling protocols to create a secure connection between your device and a remote server."

- Rating: 3 / 5
- Comments: Uses technical terms without explaining them. Does not clearly state why a VPN is useful or what benefit the user gets.

## Model Answer B

"A VPN is like a secure tunnel for your internet. When it is on, your internet traffic goes through a safe connection to another server first, which hides your real IP address and makes it harder for websites or your provider to track what you do online."

- Rating: 5 / 5
- Comments: Clear analogy (“secure tunnel”), simple language, explains the main benefit (hides IP, reduces tracking). Better aligned with non-technical users.
